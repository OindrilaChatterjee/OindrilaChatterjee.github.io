Welcome to my personal website!

I am a PhD Candidate in the Electrical and Systems Engineering Department at Washington University in St.Louis (WashU), Missouri. I work in the Adaptive Integrated Microsystems Lab (AIMLAB), advised by Professor Shantanu Chakrabartty. My research lies at the intersection of optimization, machine learning, dynamical systems and analog computing theory.

Prior to joining WashU, I did my Bachelors in Electrical Engineering from Jadavpur University in Kolkata, India, where I worked on novel Computer Vision techniques applied to biometric authentication systems with Professor Amitava Chatterjee.
